## Nhost Login

`nhost login` allows us to login to our accounts so that projects associated with them are available to be initialized and started.

```bash
nhost login
Enter your email: nunopato@gmail.com

✔ Email sent to nunopato@gmail.com, please follow the instructions within it

⠼ Waiting for your confirmation

# Check your inbox and follow the instructions

✔ Email Confirmed! You are now logged in.
```

A corresponding `nhost logout` account is also available.
